# Beyoncé – Coachella (2018)

**Date:** April 14, 2018  
**Venue:** Coachella Festival, Indio, CA  
**Identifier:** LC-0005  

This performance is celebrated for its tribute to HBCU culture, large-scale choreography, and musical direction. It is regarded as one of the most influential festival performances of the 21st century.

### Provenance
- Verified through Netflix’s *Homecoming* documentary and Coachella’s official setlist.
