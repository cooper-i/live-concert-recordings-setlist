# Nirvana – Live at Reading (1992)

**Date:** August 30, 1992  
**Venue:** Reading Festival, Reading, England  
**Identifier:** LC-0002  

This concert is often cited as one of the greatest grunge-era performances and a key moment in Nirvana’s history. Kurt Cobain’s entrance in a wheelchair became one of the most memorable openings in rock.

### Provenance
- Verified through the official 2009 DVD release and archival setlist records.
